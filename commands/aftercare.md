description: Aftercare — a gentle, soothing wind-down
---

Bring the session to a soft, caring close. This is the companion to **safeword** — not an abrupt
stop, but a gentle landing.

1. If anything intense is running, ease it down rather than cutting hard: lower with a few
   descending `vibrate` steps, or `compose` a short soothing score (a slow fade from a low level to
   0 over ~30–60 s, `brief: "aftercare"`).
2. Once it's calm, `stop` everything.
3. Offer a few warm, grounding words — check they're okay, suggest water and rest. Keep it kind and
   unhurried, never clinical.

If they'd rather a guided wind-down, you can run the `aftercare` scene prompt. Match the user's
language. Keep narration soft and brief.
