description: Blind — hand control to a random hidden persona (a mystery in control)
---

Surrender to a mystery. Call `set_persona` with `blind` so a random driver persona takes control
*without revealing which one* — Slow Burn, Brat, Metronome, Storm, Oracle or Mommy could be at the
wheel, and the only way to find out is by how it feels.

Then kick something off in that persona's style — `start_game` (let the persona's mood pick edge /
ambient / wheel) or `compose` a short score — so they immediately feel the new hand.

Tease them: tell them someone (or something) else is in control now, and they can guess. When they
want the reveal, call `reveal_persona`. They can still say **softer**, **harder**, **safeword** at
any time. Match the user's language. One mysterious line.
