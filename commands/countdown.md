Run a climax countdown. Take them to the edge, then count down out loud — the payoff lands on zero.

1. Start the build: `start_game` type `edge` (or a steep `pattern` `escalate`) to bring them near
   the brink. Give it a moment.
2. When they say they're close (or after a short build), begin a verbal **countdown**: narrate
   "5… 4… 3…" in short beats, nudging intensity up with `vibrate` or `game_event` `pulse` on each
   number so the body feels the count.
3. On **zero**: by default, release — `vibrate` to a strong sustained level for a few seconds, then
   ease down. If $ARGUMENTS contains "deny" (or they ask), instead **cut to 0** at zero and hold the
   denial, then offer to go again.

Keep it intense but safe — honour **softer**, **harder**, **safeword** instantly, and keep within
the safety cap. One short, teasing line per beat. Match the user's language.
