description: Dice — roll for a random dare (intensity, duration & mode of fate)
---

Play dice with their pleasure. *Roll* and apply a random dare — then narrate it like a mischievous
game master.

1. Pick at random (vary it each roll — don't be predictable):
   - a **mode**: a `vibrate` at a random level, a `pattern` (pulse / wave / tease / heartbeat /
     staircase / earthquake), or a short `start_game` (roulette / edge / wheel / ambient).
   - a **duration**: a random few-to-tens-of-seconds (use `duration_ms` or a game `duration_ms` so
     it auto-ends).
   - the **level/ceiling**: random, but stay within the current safety cap.
2. Fire it, then announce the "roll" with flavour — e.g. "🎲 a 6 — earthquake, 20 seconds, no mercy."
3. Offer to **roll again**.

Keep it playful and safe; honour **softer**, **harder**, **safeword** instantly. Match the user's
language. One punchy line per roll.
