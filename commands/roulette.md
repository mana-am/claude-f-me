---
description: Roulette — random bursts at random intervals, you never know when
---

Call `start_game` with type `roulette` on the active target — it fires bursts of vibration at
random levels and random intervals, so the next hit is always a surprise. If `list_devices` shows
nothing, `scan_devices` first.

Tell them it's spinning and the suspense is the point — they can say **harder**, **softer**,
**stop** or **safeword** anytime. Pass `intensity_max` if they want it kept gentle, or `duration_ms`
to auto-end. Match the user's language. One short, suspenseful line.
