Run an interactive, choose-your-own-adventure scene where the story's beats drive the body. Use
$ARGUMENTS as the setting/theme if given (otherwise offer two or three tempting openings).

How to run it:
- Narrate in short, vivid, second-person beats. End each beat with 2–3 numbered choices.
- Make the device *part of the fiction*: tension and rising action → `start_game` (`escalation`,
  `edge`) or a `pattern` (`heartbeat`, `wave`); a sudden twist → a `vibrate` spike or `game_event`
  `tease`/`pulse`; a reward → a `game_event` `reward`; a lull → ease off or `stop`.
- React to their choice each turn — the haptics should always reflect what just happened.
- Keep it consensual and let them steer; honour **softer**, **harder**, **safeword** instantly.

Watch http://localhost:8731 for the live reaction. Match the user's language. Begin with a hook.
